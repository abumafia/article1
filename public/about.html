About Scientific Horizon

Our Mission

Scientific Horizon was founded in 2021 with a clear mission: to create an accessible, high-quality platform for scientific research dissemination. We believe that knowledge should be freely available to researchers, students, and curious minds worldwide.

Our Journey

2021
Foundation

Founded by Dr. Alexander Reed with a vision to create an open-access scientific publishing platform. Published first 50 articles within the first year.

2022
International Expansion

Expanded editorial board to include researchers from 15 countries. Implemented advanced peer-review system. Reached 10,000 monthly readers.

2023
Platform Enhancement

Launched advanced search and recommendation system. Introduced article analytics for authors. Partnered with 5 academic institutions.

2024
Current Status

Serving 50,000+ monthly readers worldwide. Maintaining publication standards recognized by academic communities. Continuously improving platform features.
